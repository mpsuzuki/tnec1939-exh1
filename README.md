# TNEC 1939 "Exhibit I" Searcher
---

## What is this?

This is a simple search tool for the “Exhibit I” table included in the
Investigation of Concentration of Economic Power in the Milk Industry,
conducted by the
[Temporary National Economic Committee (TNEC)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TNEC)
in 1938–1941 (pp. 3164–3178).  
The [original report](https://archive.org/details/investigationofconc07unit/)
is available on the Internet Archive.

The table lists companies acquired by the National Dairy Products
Corporation (as it was known at the time).  
Later, this table was cited in anti‑monopoly and anti‑trust discussions
in the U.S. Congress, such as
[this statement](https://www.congress.gov/80/crecb/1947/07/11/GPO-CRECB-1947-pt7-6-1.pdf)
by
[William “Wild Bill” Langer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Langer)
on July 11, 1947, in the Senate.

## Why?

The 362 acquisitions recorded for 1923–1937 are striking, but the table
contains numerous issues.  
In some cases, similarly named companies were grouped together as if they
were a single acquisition, even though they were entirely different
entities.  
In other cases, companies that were *not* acquired were mistakenly listed
as if they had been purchased.

To help examine and debug the table, this repository provides a
machine-readable CSV version and a simple search tool.
